Satoshi Token

The $SAT token honors the visionary spirit of Bitcoin's creator, Satoshi Nakamoto. As one of the first tokens to launch on Spark Chain with a free airdrop to early ecosystem participants, $SAT represents the core values of decentralization and innovation that Bitcoin was built upon.

More than just a token, $SAT serves as a cultural symbol of the Spark Chain community, encouraging participation and driving innovation across the ecosystem. All remaining tokens after the airdrop have been permanently burned, ensuring scarcity and community ownership.
